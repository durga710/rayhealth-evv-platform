# Spot 2 — Agency Owner ("Run the agency, not the spreadsheet.")

**Length:** 30s
**Word count target:** ~75
**Tone:** Direct, confident, slightly weary-then-relieved. Owner-to-owner.
**Voice direction:** Confident female or male, 40-55. Sounds like they've run an agency. NOT a generic SaaS voice.
**Channels:** LinkedIn paid + sponsored, Facebook to home-care owner segments.

---

## Voiceover

You started this agency to help people. Not to chase signatures, fix punches, or audit your own audits. RayHealth runs your operations the way a senior coordinator would — except it doesn't sleep, doesn't quit, and doesn't ask for a raise. You stay focused on care. RayHealth handles the rest. Run your agency, not the spreadsheet.

**Word count:** 60.

---

## Shot list (30s, pain → relief arc)

| Time | Visual | VO line |
|---|---|---|
| 0–4s | Owner at a cluttered desk, late evening, head in hands, glow of monitor | "You started this agency to help people." |
| 4–9s | Insert shots: missed-punch form, signature page, audit folder | "Not to chase signatures, fix punches, or audit your own audits." |
| 9–14s | Cut to RayHealth admin dashboard — clean, calm, lit screen | "RayHealth runs your operations the way a senior coordinator would —" |
| 14–17s | Quick cuts: dashboard handling alerts automatically | "— except it doesn't sleep, doesn't quit, and doesn't ask for a raise." |
| 17–22s | Wide: same owner at the same desk, now in morning light, coffee, calm | "You stay focused on care." |
| 22–26s | Caregiver clocks in on phone, owner smiles | "RayHealth handles the rest." |
| 26–30s | Logo + tagline: "Run your agency, not the spreadsheet." | (tag line VO) |

---

## Seedance prompt sketch

- **Shot 0–4s:** "A tired-looking woman in her 40s at a cluttered home office desk, head in hands, only the screen glow lighting her face. Late evening. Documentary realism. 4 second clip."

- **Shot 4–9s:** "Quick cuts of paperwork: a paper form with red ink corrections, a signature page being signed under fluorescent light, a thick audit binder with sticky notes. Cinema verité. 5 second clip."

- **Shot 9–14s:** "Clean modern dashboard UI on a 27-inch monitor in a tidy office. Soft daylight from window. Cards organize themselves in a smooth subtle animation. 5 second clip."

- **Shot 17–22s:** "Same woman from earlier, now in a sunlit home office, holding a coffee mug, smiling at her screen. Morning light. 5 second clip."

- **Shot 22–26s:** "Split screen: left side, caregiver outdoors clocking in on a phone; right side, owner at her desk seeing the check-in pop into her dashboard. 4 second clip."

---

## Music direction

Opens minor / contemplative, transitions to major / hopeful at 17s mark.
Artlist search: "uplifting corporate transition" BPM 95-110.

## Notes

The headline insight is the contrast cut at 14–17s: same owner, same desk, two different states. That single visual does more selling work than any of the copy. Resolve's split-screen + match-cut tools handle this trivially.
