# Spot 3 — Caregiver ("A workday that respects your time.")

**Length:** 30s
**Word count target:** ~70
**Tone:** Warm, real, peer-to-peer. Not corporate. Sounds like another caregiver talking to you.
**Voice direction:** Female, 25-45, naturalistic accent. Slightly conversational. Could be a real caregiver, not voice talent.
**Channels:** Facebook + Instagram Reels (audio-on), TikTok organic, home-care job-board pre-roll.

---

## Voiceover

You picked this work because you care about the people. RayHealth picks up everything else. Tap in, tap out — your visit's already documented. No clipboards. No after-hours paperwork. No fighting the app at the door. Just a clean clock-in, a clean clock-out, and your time back. RayHealth EVV. Care, the way it's supposed to be.

**Word count:** 62.

## Music direction

Reference: think coffee-commercial scoring. Artlist search: "acoustic uplifting day" BPM 80-100.

## Notes

Critical to nail real talent for this one. Stock footage will read as fake immediately on social platforms. The "picks up own child at end" beat is the emotional anchor — it shows the platform giving back personal time, not just operational efficiency. Don't cut it for length.
