## Critical compliance copy review

Per brand governance — every claim in this spot must be defensible. Verbatim language uses "**built to support** electronic visit verification and protected health information requirements" rather than "**HIPAA compliant**" or "**EVV certified**". This is intentional and required by your brand positioning. Do not let any editor "tighten" this phrase. The phrase is the legal moat.
