# RayHealthEVV Launch Ads Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

RayHealthEVV ads should feel warm, trustworthy, medical, and bold: clean operational SaaS surfaces, confident blue end frames, light clinical whitespace, ECG motion details, orange pulse accents, and no generic AI demo gloss. The visuals should imply reliable home-care workflows without pretending to be final filmed b-roll.

## Colors

- Primary blue: `#1a5fa8`
- Deep blue: `#1248a0`
- Secondary blue: `#2d7dd2`
- Pulse orange: `#f97316`
- Surface: `#f0f4f8`
- Ink navy: `#1a3a5c`
- Success: `#1F9D55`

## Typography

- Display: Nunito, bold/black for brand and headlines
- Body: DM Sans where available
- Local render fallback: Avenir Next, Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif
- Headline weight: 800-900
- Body weight: 500

## Motion

- Calm ease-in/ease-out motion
- UI states should glide, settle, and confirm
- ECG/pulse accents may breathe subtly
- End frames should hold long enough to read the URL
- No glitch effects, neon gradients, bokeh blobs, or decorative orbs

## Compliance Guardrails

- Do not imply RayHealthEVV files taxes or executes wage payments.
- Use payroll readiness, approvals, exports, or authorized payroll-provider handoff language.
- Treat EVV, GPS, audit, and 21st Cures Act claims as subject to product/legal confirmation before paid media release.
